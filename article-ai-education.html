How to use AI Effectively?

It is a big question: What is the next step to unlocking the power of AI? The answer is a technique called Prompt Engineering. It is an essential skill for all AI users.

Let's look at a simple example to illustrate the power of Prompt Engineering:

  • Example 1: When you ask your friend "give me a pen", your friend gives you a pen. You return the pen.
  • Example 2: When you ask your friend "Please, give me a pen", your friend gives you a pen, and you return it with a "Thanks". You can see the difference in communication quality between Example 1 and 2.

Simply put, Prompt Engineering is a method of communication that helps you talk to AI more effectively. When you ask a physics doubt to a Physics Professor versus a student, you notice a massive difference in the quality of the answer. The professor's answer is much more understandable. Similarly, you can assign a "role" to the AI (e.g., "Act as a Physics Professor") when you talk about a specific topic to get expert-level responses.

Additionally, modern AI models allow you to upload PDFs, images, and other files. While not strictly necessary, providing this context helps generate much more efficient and accurate results. This feature is widely available in models like ChatGPT.

"The Art of Prompting is the future of AI—not only in the field of Education but across all industries."
